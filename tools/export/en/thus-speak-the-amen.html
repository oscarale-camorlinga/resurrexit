THUS SPEAK THE AMEN

Revelations 3,14-22

THUS SPEAK THE AMEN,
THE FAITHFUL AND TRUTHFUL WITNESS,
THE BEGINNING
OF THE CREATURES OF GOD.

I know your behavior:
you are neither hot nor cold.
I wish you were cold or hot
and not lukewarm,
because I am going
to vomit you from my mouth!

THUS SPEAK THE AMEN …

You say: «I am rich, I lack nothing».
And you are not aware
that you are wretched,
worthy of compassion,
poor, blind and naked.

I counsel you to buy from me
gold refined by fire
white garments
and eye-salve
so that you may see.

THUS SPEAK THE AMEN …

Those I love I correct;
be then zealous and repent,
listen to my voice, listen to my voice.

Behold: I am at the door and knock
if anyone hears my voice
and opens the door,
I will enter his house
and I will dine with him and he with me.

THUS SPEAK THE AMEN …
